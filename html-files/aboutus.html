About Us

Sreekara Greencity Developers is a well known & established Real Estate development firm based in Rajahmundry.

Sreekara Greencity Developers was established in 2011 by professionals & Dynamic personals.

The Developers are adequtely experienced. The Developers are from Professionals background and prior to establishing
Sreekara Greencity Developers, they have worked in the MNC sector for more than 18 years.

Sreekara Greencity Developers have identified Rajahmundry, as the prime location to start their ventures, as this location is central with all the amenities within an equal distance between vizag and vijayawada. This vision paid off their dividends in the coming years and they have established their name by delivering quality ventures with a firm commitment of timely delivery. All ventures are being completed within 5 to 8 months.

Till date they have developed 2 ventures in Rajahmundry and suburbs alone and no other Developer has succeeded many ventures in a single location till date, in Rajahmundry. The success lies with the developers being professional towards their dealing with the clients and at the same time belief in quality Ventures.

Sreekara Greencity Developers till date has Developed more than 6acers and another 20 acres will be ready by the end of 2011. The Directors travel regularly to keep themselves aware of the new technology that is being used overseas and also in design elevations to give a unique look for all their ventures. They firmly believe in giving the best to their clients.

The Developers have a very good credit rating both with the Commercial Banks and also with the local private financing firms. They also have a very good image in the Social circles and are known for their commitments and ethics in their business dealing.

The first commercial venture “Chakradwara Bandham” nearer to NH-5 surrounded by GSL Medical College, GIET Engineering college and famous Nannayya University, the most sought after address now in Rajahmundry, has recently started and more than 60% of plots have moved.
